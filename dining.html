Dining in Taniti

Fresh island flavors and easy food options.

Taniti offers local seafood, familiar favorites, and convenient grocery choices for visitors staying a few days or longer.

Local cuisine

Seafood and fish with rice

Five of Taniti’s restaurants serve mostly local fish and rice. These meals reflect the island’s location and provide visitors with a classic local dining option.

Restaurant choices

TypeCountNotes
Local fish and rice5Island-style dishes built around seafood and local staples

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants in total, offering enough variety for different travel styles and meal preferences.
Scenic meals

Dining near the water

Many visitors spend time near Yellow Leaf Bay and other scenic areas, making Taniti a great place to pair meals with beach views and relaxed island surroundings.

Grocery and convenience options

  • 2 supermarkets for larger shopping trips
  • 2 smaller grocery stores for quick essentials
  • 1 convenience store open 24 hours a day

These options make it easy for families, longer-stay travelers, and visitors in rentals or bed and breakfasts to buy snacks, drinks, and simple supplies.
