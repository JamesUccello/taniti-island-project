Getting here and getting around

Transportation options for every type of traveler.

Most visitors arrive by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night each week.

Arrival by air

Small airport service

Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. The airport is in the process of expanding so larger jets can land in the next few years.

Car rental

Rental cars near the airport

Visitors who want to explore more of the island can rent cars from a local rental agency near the airport.

Ground transit

Public and private buses

Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day, while private buses serve the rest of the island.
