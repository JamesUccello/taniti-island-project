Welcome to Taniti

Your guide to a tropical island escape.

Taniti is a small island in the Pacific with sandy and rocky beaches, a safe harbor, lush rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small active volcano. This prototype helps visitors find the island details they need quickly.

Island terrain

More than one kind of beach

Taniti offers both sandy and rocky beaches, giving visitors different ways to enjoy the coastline. Travelers can relax near white sand areas or explore the island’s more rugged shorelines.
