Places to stay

Choose the lodging style that fits your trip.

Taniti offers a wide variety of accommodations, from an inexpensive hostel to one large four-star resort, plus family-owned hotels and bed and breakfasts.

Lodging overview

  • Inexpensive hostel
  • One large four-star resort
  • Many small family-owned hotels
  • A growing number of bed and breakfasts
All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.
Local stays

Family hotels and island neighborhoods

Many visitors may prefer smaller family-owned hotels and neighborhood-style lodging for a more personal island experience close to beaches and local areas.

Comfortable option

Bed and breakfast stays

Taniti has a growing number of bed and breakfasts that can appeal to couples and first-time visitors looking for a relaxed, personalized place to stay.
